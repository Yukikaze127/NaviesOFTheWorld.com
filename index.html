Welcome Aboard, Captain!

Discover the ships, sailors, and stories that shaped the world's navies.

Navies of the World is your encyclopedia for everything naval—explore legendary ships, learn about different classes and nations, and dive into the history of maritime warfare from ancient times to the modern era. Whether you want to read detailed articles, watch documentaries, or simply browse ship galleries, you’ll find it all here.

Use the navigation above to start your journey through the fascinating world of naval history. From mighty battleships and aircraft carriers to the unsung heroes of the sea, every vessel has a story to tell.

Featured Ships

USS Iowa BB-61

Say Hello to the USS Iowa! This retired WWII and Korean War battleship, decommissioned in 1990, now serves as a museum in Los Angeles, where you can tour her decks and explore her history.

IJN Yamato

Say Konichiwa to IJN Yamato! The largest battleship ever built, she served in WWII and eventually sank. Today, she lives on in history, remembered for her legendary story.

KMS Bismarck

Say Hallo to KMS Bismarck! This powerful German battleship served in WWII and eventually sank, leaving behind a legacy of naval history and legendary battles.
